Fertilizer Application Guide

Understanding NPK Ratios

Nitrogen (N)

Promotes leaf growth and green color

Phosphorus (P)

Supports root development and flowering

Potassium (K)

Enhances overall plant health

A fertilizer labeled 10-10-10 contains 10% nitrogen, 10% phosphorus, and 10% potassium by weight.

When to Fertilize

  • Early spring when plants begin active growth
  • Mid-season for heavy feeders like vegetables
  • Fall for trees and shrubs (with low nitrogen)

Application Tips

  • Water plants before fertilizing to prevent root burn
  • Follow package instructions for proper dosage
  • Wear gloves when handling chemical fertilizers
